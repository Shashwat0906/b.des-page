Our Curriculum

Year 1 serves as the foundation for any design student, focusing on design fundamentals, creative problem-solving, and societal connections, while blending practical skills with cultural awareness and community engagement.

Learning
  • Self and Society
  • Art Fundamentals -I & II
  • Digital Creativity - I
  • Design Fundamentals -I & II
  • Fabrication Lab / Workshop
  • Fundamentals of System Thinking
  • Principles of Design
  • Technology and Policy

Year 2 delves deeper into communication design, focusing on graphic design, concept art, and cultural symbolism. Students refine their skills in visual storytelling, typography, and production techniques.

Learning
  • Art Fundamentals -I & II
  • Design Fundamentals -I & II
  • Fabrication Lab / Workshop
  • Digital Creativity - I
  • Photography and imaging
  • History of Design I

Year 3 in communication design focuses on advanced User Research, design management, andvisualization. Students build portfolios and explore immersive technologies, preparing for professional design careers.

Learning
  • Design-preneurship strategies
  • Portfolio Development
  • Design management
  • Photography and imaging
  • Visual Story Telling
  • Advertising

During the 7th Semester, you will learn practical execution of Ideas, industry level guidance and will be prepared for the industry.

The whole 8th Semester is a Hands-on practical experience via Paid Internship.
